A short story behind my work

Back to Main Page

I am a sculptor and restorer from Belgrade, Serbia, with a degree in sculpture and restoration. My work spans from classical restoration of historical architecture to contemporary sculptural and design projects.

Over the years I have worked on monumental and detailed projects, from repairing damaged ornaments and stone figures to creating conceptual forms in metal, clay, and composite materials.

My approach is rooted in craftsmanship, precision, and curiosity for material. I enjoy working at the intersection of art and construction, where creativity meets technical logic.
